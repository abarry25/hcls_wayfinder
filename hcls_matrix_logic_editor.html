Scoring logic: how it works, and how to change it

The rule

Each founder self-rates Concept, Testing, and Traction from 1–5. Whatever milestones they drag into the "30 days" column earn points toward each dimension, based on the table below. Points add directly onto the self-rating.

target = self-rating + (sum of points from 30-day milestones), capped at 5

"Not right now" means that milestone earns 0 points for that dimension, no matter the self-rating — it's simply the wrong lever for that part of the founder's readiness.

Only the "30 days" column scores anything right now. Semester and time-at-Harvard milestones are shown to the founder but don't affect the projection — that was an explicit decision, not an oversight.
